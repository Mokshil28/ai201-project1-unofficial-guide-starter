UNCC is commuter sure but there's still tons of activities on campus. Personally I wouldn't put myself into double the debt for VT and would go with UNCC but that's me. I picked UNCC because it was great for business and I could save tons of $.

You can still have that student life, but you need to go out of your way more for it I'd say. Go make friends and go to events. Parties aren't HUGE but it's not like there isn't any. There's so much to do in Charlotte that most just go out in the city which imo is better than parties.

TLDR: still have student life but you'll need to go out of your way for it. I'd pick UNCC any day but especially bc VT is double the cost, it wouldn't even be an consideration for me.

I also thought of VT. My friend goes there and it looks fun but I honestly like CLT more since I’m from VA. On weekends I tend to go out with friends maybe parties or live band performances and go out to side quest a lot. It’s not a commuter school anymore as most commuters live 5-10 mins away from campus and it got that name due to the fact a while ago many still lived at home (much further) and would drive to campus for class and then go back home. Most people I know live nearby now. Greek life is great and the girls I know in sororities love it! Def weigh out your options and find things to do at VT bc idk if they have as much. The town isn’t really that interesting imo compared to CLT bc we have a shit ton of things to do, but the area of VT is gorgeous! Weigh your options
