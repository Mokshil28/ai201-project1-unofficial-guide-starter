I went to UNCC for CS. I work at a major bank with other UNCC grads and many Chapel Hill grads~ so, in terms of real life, there isn’t much of a difference. Personal opinion- Chapel Hill is very well known, so the prestige is there. One thing I appreciated about UNCC is that it has the distinction of CCI as well as the “concentrations”. What I had heard about the UNC Chapel Hill program is that many people struggled to get credits for higher level classes due to how limited the seats/times were. This may not be the case now (class of 2023) As far as campus life goes, I believe UNCC is more of a commuter school; I participated in a handful of student organizations but I never lived on campus or in student housing.

Another thing that may help you make a decision is career prospects. Charlotte has a lot of tech and banking opportunities. Raleigh/Durham area is more of health and research focus (with some tech companies scattered around)

Either school will get you what you need- I also think being in North Carolina is already a plus for this degree, with many companies setting sites on building offices or headquarters.
