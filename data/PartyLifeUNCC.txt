How is the social life at UNCC? Is it really dead on the weekends?
Academic

I keep hearing that its a BIG commuter campus. and practically dead on the weekends. Personally, I always thought it being dead was an appeal. Should you want to fully make use of a campus’s resources, then the weekend is the time to do so. Library, dining halls/other restaurants, gym, etc That may be the core few, but don’t sleep on how much easier that makes life on campus. If you live on campus, it is especially easier since you can enjoy campus without the hustle and bustle. Likewise, you’ll be able to more easily do laundry and, if you have roommates, there is a decent likelihood they will be among the few departing on weekends. Don’t forget, there is still a city and still people who stay on or near campus on the weekends. Not everyone wants to spend their free weekends with their family.
